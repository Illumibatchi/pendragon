## How we'll use this vault
- You paste a session summary.
- I create/update:
  - a session note in `01 - Sessions/`
  - character pages in `02 - Characters/` (use `_templates/Character Template.md`)
  - timeline entries in `00 - Hub/Timeline.md`
  - any needed place/faction pages in `03 - Places & Factions/`

## Conventions
- **Session notes** are organized by *real date* and tagged with the **in-game year**.
- **Characters/Places/Factions** get their own pages and are linked from sessions + timeline.
