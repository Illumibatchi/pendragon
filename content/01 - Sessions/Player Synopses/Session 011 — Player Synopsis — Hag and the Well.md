## Millicent returns (Madoc + white stag vision)
Millicent catches up to the Kenroi. She reports:
- Prince [[Sir Madoc]] gravely injured by a manticore
- a woman dressed in white riding a **white stag with glowing antlers**
- Millicent was accompanied by [[Brother Maynard]] (priest) while bringing Madoc to safety

## Hag chamber
- Sir Kenian leads into rooms; loud snoring.
- Liam puts a dagger to a sleeping old woman’s throat; she does not stir.
- Millicent yells; the hag opens one eye — **goat eyes**. Liam freezes in terror.
- Hag claws at Kenian; Kenian’s sword hits like striking a tree.
- A druid appears in the corridor.
- As the hag lunges for Lycus, Lycus spears her through the heart; a snake-like tongue unrolls.
- Liam severs the hag’s head.

## Druid fight
- The druid tries glamour on Millicent; she resists.
- Lycus bashes the druid with a flaming quarterstaff; Aspara hits from behind; Kenian strikes; Millicent finishes.
- Liam opens the hag’s locked “underwear chest” and claims it.

## War-room map
They find sleeping mats and a table with wine skins and a gladius. A map accurately shows Uther’s troop dispositions — evidence of spying. They take it.

## Ambush room
Double doors open into ambush: kerns, cambrians, bandits behind barricades.
- Kenian and Liam lead.
- Millicent strikes over Kenian’s shoulder to mortally wound the last kern blocking the corridor.
- Bandit archers in corners; two cambrian longbowmen in a side corridor; they are all cleared.

## Merlin in a cage
They find Merlin/Merdwyl caged. He warns of “red sorceresses” and urges them to leave. They ignore him.

## Red cultists → undead → sorceress
- Red cultists at a tight T-intersection; Millicent squeezes in to engage; Kenian chops one’s head; Lycus/Aspara flank and finish.
- Then undead knights appear (knightly tabards), and a breathtakingly beautiful woman.
- Liam is entranced, thinks she’s an angel; snaps back when bitten.
- Spears seem ineffective; Liam’s sword works.
- Kenian becomes entranced; Aspara knocks the sorceress out from behind.

They bag her head and bring her to Merlin. Merlin says they captured him to interrupt the King’s plans for Britain.

## The flooded shrine and the Lady
They find a flooded ancient shrine with a well and a pile of treasure. They explore a soot-covered smithy with strange four-toed tracks; follow them toward a hatchery and suspect **Dverga**.

Back at the well:
- Kenian asks for his horse; the lady says she doesn’t have it.
- Liam asks for **fame and success**; the lady offers it for a price: Liam must return one day before his death and give back what was granted.
- Liam accepts; takes a chest of rubies.
