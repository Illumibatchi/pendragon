# Session 012 — Player Synopsis — Dunkerton / Peace of Summerland

> Linked chronicle page: [[Session 012 - The Burning of Dunkerton and the Peace of Summerland]]

## Sir Geraint
Sir Geraint, sole survivor of Knight Commander Caradoc’s conroi, recounts Dunkerton: abandoned like Wells with food offerings; Caradoc burns it per Uther’s orders; a giant ambushes and slaughters the conroi; Geraint escapes but barely remembers how.

## Abort the burn-orders
The Kenroi debates continuing the Summerland “swear or burn” orders:
- Millicent wants to continue
- Drustin/Liam/Geraint want to abort
- Kenian decides to abort and head for Salisbury

## The children of Shepton
At a crossroads they find **13 ragged children** with the knights’ missing horses. When Liam claims his horse, a child replies: “**But sir, he led me here**.” They say they came from **Shepton** through the marshes. Brother Maynard volunteers to escort them to an abbey.

## Sorceress swap at Woodhouse
At Woodhouse they show the prisoner cage — inside is **Brother Maynard**. The red sorceress has apparently switched places with him and escaped. The group fears she will use the children as agents against Salisbury.

## Dead on the road
They find an overturned wagon with a partially eaten peasant family; Geraint judges the wounds were inflicted by men. They bury the dead.

## Wolves near Vagon
They reach Vagon by night. Drustin insists on a shortcut deer-path toward Tisbury; Millicent/Liam/Geraint accompany him. Wolves ambush (pack of 5). Drustin and Liam are left near death; Millicent’s treatment and leadership gets them back to safety.

## Peace of Summerland
Uther meets King Cadwy alone in a pavilion and emerges pale but with a signed treaty. Terms mostly restore prior peace, but Cadwy is granted new lands and the title **Count of Somerset**. Uther grants his ward **Lady Ellen** to **Count Roderick** in marriage.

## Legal oddity
With Aurelius dead and Uther not yet elected High King, the Kenroi are in a legal limbo: not formally Uther’s household knights and not sworn to Roderick (exception: Geraint is sworn to Roderick).

## Rubies + ransom
Liam is furious Uther has not paid Eosa’s ransom and is legally owed one-third of Liam’s rubies.
