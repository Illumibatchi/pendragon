# Liam

## Quick facts
- **Description:** A prudent, dutiful squire known for supporting his knight and tracking vital equipment and supplies.

## Timeline
- **(480)** — Introduced as a squire of Salisbury. *(Source: [[Session 000 - The Young Squires of Salisbury]])*
- **(480)** — Wounds a Saxon in the Purdue Forest ambush after calling on his honor. *(Source: [[Session 003 - The Empty Castle and the Forest Ambush]])*
- **(480)** — Jousts at Winchbank after provoking a challenge; is eventually knocked from his horse (opponent later revealed as Lady Ellen in disguise). *(Source: [[Session 004 - The Lady’s Secret and the Feast at Winchbank]])*
- **(480)** — Buries his silver dagger (a gift from Ellen) as part of an offering to appease the fair folk after the fairy dragon’s death. *(Source: [[Session 005 - The Fairy Dragon and the Ogre’s Return]])*
- **(481)** — Receives the [[Shield of St. Crispin]] from Drustin; attempts to lift the High King onto a horse during the wyvern crisis; the shield saves him from certain death. *(Source: [[Session 006 - The Shield of St. Crispin and the Fall of the Wyvern]])*
- **(482)** — Bargains with the [[Lady of the Well]] for fame and success and takes a chest of rubies, owing a future return. *(Source: [[Session 011 - The Hag of the Passage and the Lady of the Well]])*
- **(482)** — Furious Uther has not paid King Eosa’s ransom; additionally, by law the king is entitled to one-third of Liam’s rubies. *(Source: [[Session 012 - The Burning of Dunkerton and the Peace of Summerland]])*
- **(484)** — Tells Drimant about the Lady-of-the-Well bargain; warned it may be demonic and the rubies should be returned. *(Source: [[Session 015 - The Road to York and the Ambush in Sherwood]])*
