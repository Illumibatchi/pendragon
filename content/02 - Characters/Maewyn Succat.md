
> Christian priest freed from a pit near Wilderspool.

## Summary
- A ragged, half-starved Christian priest imprisoned in a deep pit near Wilderspool’s north tower.
- Claimed he was jailed for attempting to exorcise a serpent-eyed woman.
- Freed by the company and accompanied them toward the [[Wyrd Pool]].
- Charged the red-haired priestess with reckless faith, fighting with a staff despite weakness.
- Delivered the final blow that slew the serpent creature (with his staff).
