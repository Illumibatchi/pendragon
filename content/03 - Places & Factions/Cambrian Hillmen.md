# Cambrian Hillmen

## Notes
Hill tribes/skirmishers from Cambria. Identified by long dart-like missiles; they ambush the squires in the Forest of Gloom and maintain a cannibal camp. *(See: [[Session 002 - Wolves, Hillmen, and the Ogre’s Trail]])*
