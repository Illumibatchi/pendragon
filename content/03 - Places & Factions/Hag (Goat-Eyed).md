# Hag (Goat-Eyed)

## Notes
An old woman found snoring in a chamber; reveals goat eyes and fights with unnatural strength (Kenian’s blade sounds like hitting wood). Killed by Lycus’ spear through the heart; Liam severs the head. A snake-like tongue spills from her mouth at death.
