# Ladies of the Lake (rumor)

## Notes
White-cloaked women are sometimes associated with the Ladies of the Lake—an ancient, mysterious order tied to sacred sites (as recalled by [[Noah]] after the sighting of [[The Woman in White (Silchester)]])
