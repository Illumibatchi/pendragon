# River Foss

## Overview
One of two rivers that flow through [[Eboracum (York)]] (along with the River Ouse). The Foss runs through the city walls via a drainage system, which presents a potential strategic weakness for infiltration or assault.

## Notes
In the campaign year 484, Millicent's conroi consulted fortification plans at the [[Bath Scriptorium]] and identified a vulnerability: the drain where the River Foss passes through York's walls could serve as an entry point for infiltrators or attackers.
