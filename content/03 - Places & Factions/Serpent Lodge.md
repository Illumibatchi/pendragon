# Serpent Lodge

## Notes
A healers’/surgeons’ guild operating inside [[Wilderspool]]. In truth, its members were serpent-things tied to the Wyrd Pool cult.

## Timeline
- **(484)** — Confronted by the conroi; the healers transform into serpent-people and call forth a new manifestation of the serpent goddess. The lodge is destroyed and the serpent goddess slain (final blow by [[Maewyn Succat]]). [[Ælflaed]] dies in the battle. *(Source: [[Session 018 - The Serpent Lodge and the Fall of Ælflaed]])*
